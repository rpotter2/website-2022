About Me

I'm Rachel Potter, I've lived in the Richmond area for most of my life, but now I'm a senior at University of Mary Washington majoring in Communication and Digital Studies, with a minor in Computer Science.

I've always been a creative person with a lot of ideas floating around in my head and love learning and exploring different ways to express my passions. It was during quarantine where I discovered a love for programming and how it allows one to turn intangible ideas into reality. I've most enjoyed creating websites and being able to curate user experiences from all angles.

In class I've learned desktop software and backend development while my frontend skills have been all self-taught. I've worked mainly in Javascript, Python, and Java but can quickly learn any language or program.
